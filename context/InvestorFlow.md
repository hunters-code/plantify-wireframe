7. Risk Management untuk Investor
Investment Risks
1. Startup Execution Risk
Founder gagal execute business plan
Market conditions berubah drastically
Operational challenges tidak teratasi
Competition lebih kuat dari expected
Mitigation: Diversify across multiple startups, thorough due diligence
2. Profit Sharing Risk
Startup tidak generate profit sesuai projection
Founder tidak bayar profit sharing tepat waktu dalam ckUSDC
Business pivot yang affect profitability
Mitigation: Multi-layer protection (founder ckUSDC collateral + platform reserve + community governance)
3. Platform Risk
Technical issues dengan smart contracts
Platform discontinue operations
Regulatory changes affecting operations
Mitigation: Platform audit, regulatory compliance, backup recovery plans
4. Liquidity Risk
NFT tidak bisa dijual selama 36 bulan
No exit option jika ingin keluar lebih awal
Capital terikat untuk jangka menengah
Mitigation: Only invest money yang tidak butuh untuk 3+ years

General Investment Questions
Q: Berapa minimum investment di Plantify? A: Minimum 1 NFT per startup. Harga bervariasi tergantung startup, typically $50-500 ckUSDC per NFT.

Q: Bagaimana jika startup gagal total? A: Ada triple protection: founder ckUSDC collateral, platform reserve, dan community governance. Worst case scenario, Anda dapat proportional share dari remaining funds.
Q: Apakah profit sharing guaranteed? A: Tidak guaranteed, tapi ada multiple safeguards. Founder sudah setor ckUSDC collateral 12 bulan profit sharing sebelum launch.

Q: Bagaimana cara track portfolio performance? A: Platform provide dashboard dengan real-time tracking semua investments dalam ckUSDC, returns, voting history.
Risk & Legal Questions

Q: Bagaimana tax implications? A: Profit sharing dalam ckUSDC adalah taxable income. Consult tax advisor untuk specific situation Anda.
Q: Bagaimana dengan currency stability? A: ckUSDC provides stability compared to volatile cryptocurrencies, making returns predictable dan easier untuk tax planning.
